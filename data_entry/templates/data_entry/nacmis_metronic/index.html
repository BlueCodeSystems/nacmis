Welcome to the National HIV/AIDS/STI/TB Council Management Information System (NACMIS).

NACMIS provides a holistic picture for the HIV and AIDS multisectoral response in Zambia targeting not only decision-makers across all levels but also the public. This is a platform that officially triangulates the National HIV and AIDS programmatic data with social-determinants, behavior change and clinical data sets in line with the National HIV and AIDS Strategic Framework 2017-2021 in order to develop a culture of HIV and AIDS prevention data for decision-making at all levels of government and partner interventions.

Thank you for partnering in the response to HIV/AIDS.
