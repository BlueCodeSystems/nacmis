Welcome to the National HIV/ADIS/STI/TB Council Management Information System (NACMIS)

NACMIS provides a holistic picture for the HIV/AIDS response in Zambia for not only decision-makers across all levels but also the public. This is a platform that officially triangulates the National HIV/AIDS programmatic data with social-determinant, behavior change and clinical data sets in line with the National HIV/AIDS Strategic Framework 2017-2021 in order to develop a culture of HIV/AIDS prevention data for decision-making at all levels of government and partner interventions.

Thank you for partnering in the response to HIV/AIDS.
